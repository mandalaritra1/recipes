## Method

- Grate gajor
- Korai te ghee debo
- Tate theto kora elach debo
- Add dry fruits (kaju, vijiye rakha almond, but Rona prefers badam)
- Halka ektu fry kore gajor diye debo ote
- Pinch of salt, fry it for next 15 minutes; jokhon gajorer kancha gondho chole geche, aar ghee er vaja gondho uthte suru koreche, tar poreo 5 min moto vajbo (total ~15 min)
- Gajor would shrink to around 60% of its original volume
- Dudh add korbo (100 gm gajorer jonne roughly 200–300 gm)
- Aste aste reduce hote debo, jokhon reduce hoye eseche, add chini
- Add guro kora elach, keshar gola dudh, aar kichu keshar string
- Rona prefers it a bit runny


## Notes

- Rona prefers laal gajor for its color
