## Ingredients

- কচু শাক
- ইলিশ মাছের মাথা
- পাঁচফোড়ন, শুকনো লঙ্কা, তেজপাতা
- চেরা কাঁচা লঙ্কা
- হলুদ, লবণ
- অল্প চিনি (half teaspoon)
- তেল

## Method

- first boil the kochu sag in salt water, then drain the water and keep only the saag
- rinse it off in running water
- boil it for 10 minutes
- then place a korai on the oven, add oil
- fry the Ilish Mach er matha — medium fry
- then keep it aside
- add panch foron, sukno lonka, tej pata in the same oil
- add some chera kancha lonka as well
- then add the boiled kochu saag, holud and Lobon
- mix it well
- cook/fry it for 10 minutes
- then, add the fried head of Hilsa
- mix it, make it tukro tukro with khunti
- mix it well in the saag
- add some chini, half-teaspoon
- cook it for another 10 minutes

## Notes

- very little chini, otherwise kaku will go in high shouting mode
- serve with steaming hot rice and patilebu (dark green) — not the fake yellow ones with no smell
